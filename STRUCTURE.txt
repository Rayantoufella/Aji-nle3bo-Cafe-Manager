📊 FLUX DU ROUTING

Navigateur
    ↓
http://localhost/Aji-nle3bo-Cafe-Manager/games
    ↓
.htaccess (mod_rewrite)
    ↓
public/index.php?url=games
    ↓
Charge autoload + constantes
    ↓
router/routes.php (déclare routes)
    ↓
router->run()
    ↓
Cherche route GET /games
    ↓
Exécute callback 'GameController@index'
    ↓
app/controller/GamesController.php
    ↓
class GameController
    ↓
$instance->index()
    ↓
require views/games/index.php
    ↓
Affiche la liste des jeux
